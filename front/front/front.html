STANDARDIZING CURRICULUM

Mission

AICTE approved courses in the field of Engineering and Technology, Management, Pharmacy, Architecture etc are affiliated to different Universities and are conducted as per the syllabus prescribed by these Universities. There are more than 100 such universities in the Country affiliating the same course(s). However, the structure of the course, content and scheme of the course differs widely from University to University, resulting in variation in the academic standards of the same degree approved by AICTE at the time of admission to higher studies and employment in India and abroad. Therefore our service helps to resolve this issue by mapping structure, content and scheme of the courses at present run by affiliated Universities and map important features and compare with a standard curriculum of highest standard. This would help AICTE to develop and enforce uniform academic standards all over the country for all its approved Courses.





Services


Content, Scheme and Structure Mapping

Our application maps the content, scheme and structure of your given University curriculum to the standard curriculum of the highest standard.

Syllabus comparison

After mapping the content, scheme and structure, our application compares the syllabus of your given University curriculum to the standard curriculum

Syllabus Approval

After comparing the syllabus of your given university with that of the common curriculum of the highest standard, we give the conclusion based on the minimum percentage of the syllabus matching with the common curriculum. If it passes that minimum percentage, the syllabus of your university will be appproved.

Suggestions

We also give you suggestions of important topics or chapters that you may have missed out or may want to include in your curriculum
